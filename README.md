Wisdom @ Work is a Lahore-based HR consultancy website that connects small and medium enterprises with comprehensive people solutions. The platform showcases end-to-end HR services backed by 27+ years of senior HR leadership experience.
Key Features

Service Portfolio Showcase: Executive recruitment, manpower outsourcing, training needs analysis, organizational development, succession planning, and performance management systems
Structured Approach: Four-step engagement process (Discovery → Strategy Design → Implementation → Ongoing Support)
Multi-page Architecture: Home, About, Services, Our Approach, Why Us, and Contact sections
Contact Integration: Phone, email, and location details with contact form
Responsive Design: Mobile-friendly interface for accessibility across devices
Newsletter Subscription: Email capture for HR insights and organizational development updates

Core Services

Executive Recruitment & Talent Acquisition
Manpower Outsourcing Solutions
Training Needs Analysis (TNA)
Organizational Development (OD)
Succession Planning
Career Planning & Development
Performance Management Systems

Target Audience
Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) seeking practical, cost-effective HR solutions aligned with business realities and organizational culture.
